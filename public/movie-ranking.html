inda's K-Drama Recommendations

Top 3 Korean series all time according to inda

Reply 1988

Definitely should watch! You guys, this is one of the og k-drama that really suites all of people, all ages, and a good one foo the k-drama-beginner. The point of view, the story, the messages, all hit. The background story is about a simple life of families that live together and make the most of it.

Prison Playbook

The series is about life of prisoner, their different personalities and different background stories really got my heart. Should watch! Thank Me later :) Oh prepare your tissues.

Hospital Playlist

This!! The story is about life as hospital/health worker, 5 bestfriends work together. The most fun to watch. Light but no boring! A dream friend group for sure.
